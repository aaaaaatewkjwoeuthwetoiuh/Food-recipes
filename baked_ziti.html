what you need

  • 1 lb ziti pasta
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb italian sausage
  • chopped large onion
  • 1 tbsp minced rosemary
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • half a teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • marinara sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • half lb mozzarella grated or shreded
  • 1 C of ricotta
  • 1 C of parmeasan

steps

  1. heat large pot of salted water 2 qt water = 1 tbsp salt
  2. boil
  3. drain pasta using colander (toss with small amount of olive oil)
  4. while water is heating start on sauce
  5. heat tbsp of olive oil in large saute pan on medium heat
  6. when oil is hot add sausage
  7. break up chunks of sausage as cooks
  8. sprinkle with little salt
  9. when meat browned add onions and stir
  10. saute for 4-5 mins
  11. add garlic,rosemary or basil,italian seasoning and red pepper flakes and stir to combine
  12. cook for 1 minute
  13. add tomato sauce and stir well
  14. bring to simmer
  15. add salt and pepper
  16. Preheat in oven to 350°F
  17. Spread layer of sauice in bottom of 9x13 in casserole pan
  18. put half of ricotta cheese
  19. add spoonful of sauce to pasta
  20. stir
  21. add pasta to casserole
  22. Freeze pasta
  23. Bake in oven at 350°F for 20 to 25 mins
