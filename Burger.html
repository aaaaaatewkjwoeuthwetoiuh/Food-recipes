what you need

  • Burger patty
  • Burger buns
  • Stove
  • Pan
  • Plate
  • Spatula

steps

  1. Get your items ready
  2. Put your stove on high
  3. Put your pan on the stovetop
  4. Put burger patty on pan
  5. Grill for 5 mins
  6. Use your spatula to flip and grill other side for 5 more minutes
  7. Put your patty on your plate
  8. Put your two buns on the pan
  9. Grill for 5 mins
  10. Put buns on plate
  11. Put patty between buns
  12. enjoy!
