what you need

  • 2 bread
  • cheese square
  • pan
  • stove
  • plate

steps

  1. Get your things ready
  2. Put your stove on medium
  3. Put your pan on stove top
  4. Put your bread on the pan
  5. Toast for 5 minutes or til toasty
  6. Put your toast on plate
  7. Put your cheese square in between the bread
  8. Put your sandwich on the stovetop
  9. Cook til cheese melty enough
  10. Put sandwich on plate
  11. enjoy!
