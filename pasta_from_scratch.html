what you need

  • 1 Beaten egg
  • half teaspoon salt
  • 1 C all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp water

steps

  1. in medium sized bowl combine flour and salt
  2. add egg and mix
  3. lightly flour a surface
  4. knead for 3 to 4 mins with machine or hand roll dough
  5. Use machine or knife to cut dough into desired length and width
