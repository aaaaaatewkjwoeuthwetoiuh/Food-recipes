simple sandwich recipe

what you need

  • you need 2 bread
  • 1 ham
  • bottle of oil
  • pan
  • plate

steps
  1. get your items ready
  2. turn your stove on to Med
  3. put your pan on the cooktop
  4. put your bread on the pan
  5. put drizzle of oil on bread
  6. cook for 5 mins
  7. put your bread on a plate
  8. put your piece of ham on the pan
  9. cook for 5 mins or til crispy
  10. put oil on ham
  11. put the bottom bread first
  12. put ham in middle
  13. put top bread last
  14. enjoy!
